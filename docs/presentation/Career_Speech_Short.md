# Career Presentation Speech

This is a short and natural speech script for the PPT.
It is designed for about 3 minutes.

## Slide 1

Hello everyone. Today I would like to talk about career choices among college students. I chose this topic because career planning is very important for university students. It can influence our study goals, motivation, and future life. In this presentation, I will briefly introduce my survey, share two main findings, and give some suggestions.

## Slide 2

For this topic, I used a questionnaire survey. In this example, 24 college students took part in it. They came from different majors and different years of study. I also collected some simple background information, such as their major, year of study, and gender ratio. My goal was to understand what students care about most when choosing a career.

## Slide 3

In my survey, I asked several questions about career interests and career plans. For example, I asked which career field they were interested in, which factor mattered most in career choice, whether they wanted a job related to their major, and which career field they thought was the most promising in the future. These questions helped me understand both students’ preferences and their concerns.

## Slide 4

Now let’s look at the first finding. This bar chart shows the most important factor when students choose a career. We can see that passion ranked first, while job stability ranked second. Salary was also important, but family expectations had very little influence. This finding suggests that most students want a job they truly like, but at the same time they still care about having a stable future.

## Slide 5

The second finding is about promising career fields. This pie chart shows that technology was the most popular answer, followed by healthcare. In my opinion, this is probably because these two fields are developing quickly and usually provide more job opportunities. They may also seem more stable and better paid than some other fields.

## Slide 6

To conclude, this survey shows that college students try to balance personal interest and practical needs when thinking about their future careers. Based on these findings, I think schools should offer more career guidance, internship information, and career talks. Students should also explore different career options earlier and make plans step by step. Thank you for listening, and I am happy to answer any questions.

## Very Short Closing Version

Thank you for listening. That is all for my presentation, and now I am ready for your questions.
