# Career Survey Questions

You can use these questions in Wenjuanxing, Google Forms, or a class group chat.

## Background Questions

1. What is your major?
2. What is your year of study?
3. What is your gender?

## Core Questions

4. Which career field are you most interested in?
5. Which factor matters most when choosing a career?
   - Salary
   - Passion
   - Job stability
   - Family expectations
   - Other
6. Do you want a future job related to your major?
   - Yes
   - No
   - Not sure
7. Which career field seems most promising in the future?
   - Technology
   - Healthcare
   - Education
   - Business
   - Design
   - Other
8. What is your biggest worry about your future job?
   - Low salary
   - High pressure
   - Unclear career path
   - Lack of interest
   - Competition
9. How confident are you about your future career plan?
   - Very confident
   - Quite confident
   - Neutral
   - Not very confident
   - Not confident at all
10. What can schools do to help students make better career decisions?

## Quick Editing Guide

- If you already have real responses, replace the sample numbers in the PPT.
- If you need faster data collection, send the questionnaire to at least 20 classmates.
- Keep only the strongest 2 findings in the presentation so it stays within 3 minutes.
